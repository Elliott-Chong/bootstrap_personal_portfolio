System Administrator

The System Administrator manages hardware and software checks of workstations and servers. He/She ensures all standards, policies and guidelines are met. He/She reviews and reports on the amount of time the service is operational and the system performance. He/She plans and reports review status. He/She develops troubleshooting solutions for recovery and system improvement.

System Administrator
Job Requirements:
  1. Create a rollback path in the event of failure.
  2. Determine the resources necessary to carry out the changing development.
  3. Develop a plan for updates to be added to the system.
  4. Perform the changes required in system software according to organisation guidelines.
  5. Schedule system change to ensure business activities run normally.
